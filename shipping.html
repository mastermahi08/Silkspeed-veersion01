Shipping Policy

Information about our shipping process, delivery timelines, and international shipping guidelines.

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

1. Order Processing

We strive to process and ship your orders as quickly as possible:

  • Processing Time: Orders are processed within 24-48 hours during business days (Monday-Friday)
  • Weekend Orders: Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day
  • Cut-off Time: Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST will be processed the same day
  • Stock Verification: All items are verified for availability before shipping

Note: During peak seasons (holidays, sales events), processing may take 2-3 business days. We appreciate your patience.

2. Delivery Time Estimates

Delivery times vary depending on your location and selected shipping method:

Shipping Method Delivery Time Best For
Standard Worldwide Shipping 7-15 business days Regular orders, budget-friendly
Express Global Shipping 3-7 business days Faster delivery, trackable
Priority Overnight 1-2 business days Urgent orders, last-minute needs
Free Standard Shipping 7-15 business days Orders over $150 (USA only)

Please Note: Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to:

  • Customs clearance procedures
  • Local courier delays
  • High-volume seasons (holidays)
  • Weather conditions and natural events
  • Remote or rural locations

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on:

  • Destination Country: International shipping rates vary by region
  • Shipping Method: Standard, Express, or Priority
  • Order Weight & Dimensions: Based on package size
  • Order Value: Free shipping thresholds may apply

Free Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on orders over $150 to the following regions:

  • United States (all 50 states)
  • Canada (provinces with standard service)
  • United Kingdom (mainland only)

Free shipping applies to standard shipping only. Express and Priority shipping are available at additional cost.

4. Tracking Information

Once your order ships, you will receive:

  • Shipping Confirmation Email: Sent within 24 hours of dispatch
  • Tracking Number: Provided in your shipping confirmation
  • Carrier Information: Details about the shipping carrier
  • Estimated Delivery Date: Based on carrier estimates

How to Track Your Order

You can track your shipment using:

  • Email Link: Click the tracking link in your shipping confirmation
  • Our Website: Visit our Order Tracking page
  • Carrier Website: Use the tracking number on the carrier's website
  • 17Track.com: Universal tracking for multiple carriers

Note: Tracking information may take 24-48 hours to update after the carrier receives your package. International tracking updates may have additional delays.

6. Customs, Duties & Taxes

Important Information for International Customers:

Customs Duties & Import Taxes

International orders may incur:

  • Import Taxes: VAT, GST, or other sales taxes
  • Customs Duties: Fees charged by your country
  • Handling Fees: Administrative charges by customs
  • Brokerage Fees: For customs clearance services

These costs are NOT included in our product prices or shipping fees. They are determined by your country's regulations and must be paid by the recipient.

How to Estimate Customs Fees

You can estimate potential fees by:

  • Contacting your local customs office
  • Using online duty calculators
  • Checking your country's import regulations

Important: We cannot predict or control customs fees. Delays caused by customs clearance are outside our control. Please ensure you understand your country's import regulations before ordering.

7. Incorrect Shipping Information

Please double-check your shipping address before completing your order. SilkSpeed is not responsible for:

  • Failed delivery due to incorrect addresses
  • Lost packages due to wrong information provided
  • Additional shipping costs for address corrections
  • Delays caused by address verification

Address Correction

If you need to correct your shipping address:

  1. Contact us immediately at support@silkspeed.com
  2. Provide your order number and correct address
  3. We'll attempt to update the address before shipping
  4. If already shipped, contact the carrier directly

Address corrections after shipping may incur additional fees from the carrier.

8. Lost or Delayed Shipments

If your package appears to be lost or significantly delayed:

Check First:

  • Verify the tracking information is up-to-date
  • Check with neighbors or building management
  • Look for delivery attempt notices
  • Allow extra time for international shipments

If No Updates for 5-7 Days:

  1. Contact us at support@silkspeed.com
  2. Provide your order number and tracking information
  3. We will coordinate with the carrier for investigation
  4. Most carriers require 10-15 days to declare a package lost

Investigation Timeline: Carrier investigations typically take 7-14 business days. We will keep you updated throughout the process.
