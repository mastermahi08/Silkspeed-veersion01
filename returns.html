Returns & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction matters to us. Learn about our return process and refund guidelines.

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

1. Our Return Promise

At SilkSpeed Race Wear, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not fully happy with your purchase, please review our return & refund guidelines below.

We offer a 30-day return window from the date of delivery for most items. This gives you plenty of time to try on your apparel and ensure it meets your expectations.

COVID-19 Update: During the pandemic, we extended our return window to 45 days for all orders. This extension remains in effect until further notice.

2. Eligibility for Returns

We accept returns if the following conditions are met:

Items Eligible for Return

Condition Eligibility Notes
Item is unused and unworn Eligible No signs of wear or use
Original tags are intact Eligible All tags must be attached
Packaging is undamaged Eligible Original packaging in good condition
Return request within 30 days Eligible From delivery date
Proof of purchase provided Eligible Order number or receipt

Items Not Eligible for Return

Item Type Eligibility Reason
Used or damaged clothing Not Eligible Hygiene and quality reasons
Custom/OEM items Not Eligible Made to order
Clearance or final-sale products Not Eligible Marked as final sale
Items without original tags Not Eligible Cannot be resold
Personalized items Not Eligible Customized for you
Underwear & intimate apparel Not Eligible Hygiene reasons

3. Return Process Step-by-Step

Follow these simple steps to return an item:

1

Contact Us

Email us at returns@silkspeed.com within 30 days of delivery. Include:

  • Your order number
  • Item(s) you wish to return
  • Reason for return
  • Photos if applicable (damage, wrong item)
2

Receive Return Authorization

We'll review your request and send:

  • Return Authorization (RMA) number
  • Return instructions
  • Prepaid return label (US only)
  • Return address

Do not return items without an RMA number.

3

Prepare Your Return

Pack the item securely with:

  • All original tags attached
  • Original packaging if possible
  • Include the RMA number inside the package
  • Do not include accessories or gifts
4

Ship the Package

Use the provided return label or ship to our return address:

SilkSpeed Returns Department
456 Returns Avenue
Cityville, CA 12345
USA

Keep your tracking number for reference.

5

Receive Refund

Once we receive and inspect the returned item:

  • We'll email you with the inspection results
  • Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days
  • You'll receive confirmation when completed

4. Return Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs vary depending on the reason for return:

Return Reason Shipping Cost Notes
Change of mind / Size exchange Customer pays Return shipping is your responsibility
Wrong item received We cover Prepaid return label provided
Damaged or defective item We cover Prepaid return label provided
Quality issue We cover Prepaid return label provided

Important Notes:

  • Original shipping fees paid at checkout are non-refundable
  • International returns may have additional customs fees (customer's responsibility)
  • We recommend using trackable shipping and keeping proof of postage
  • Returns lost in transit without tracking are not eligible for refund

5. Refund Timelines

After receiving and inspecting the returned item:

Processing Time

  • Inspection: 2-3 business days after receiving return
  • Refund Processing: 5-10 business days after approval
  • Bank Processing: Additional 3-10 business days depending on your bank

Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days to appear on your statement
  • PayPal: 2-5 business days to appear in your PayPal account
  • Apple Pay/Google Pay: 5-10 business days to original payment method

Note: The refund timeline begins when we receive your return, not when you ship it. International returns may take longer due to customs processing.

6. Exchanges

We currently offer exchanges for different sizes or colors:

Exchange Process

  1. Submit a return for the item you wish to exchange
  2. Wait for return approval and RMA number
  3. Place a new order for the desired size or color
  4. Ship your return using the provided instructions

This two-step process ensures:

  • You receive the correct size/color quickly
  • We can reserve inventory for your exchange
  • Accurate tracking of both transactions
  • Faster processing than traditional exchanges

Exchange Shipping

For size/color exchanges:

  • You pay return shipping for the original item
  • You pay shipping for the new item
  • If the exchange is due to our error, we cover all shipping costs

7. Damaged or Wrong Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item:

Immediate Action Required

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  2. Provide photos showing the damage or wrong item
  3. Include your order details and contact information
  4. Do not use or wear the damaged/wrong item

Our Response

We will:

  • Respond within 24 hours
  • Provide a prepaid return label (if return is needed)
  • Ship the correct/replacement item immediately
  • Refund or replace at no additional cost to you

Important: For damage claims, photos must clearly show the damage and packaging. Claims made after 48 hours may not be eligible for free replacement.

8. International Returns

Additional considerations for international returns:

Customs Declarations

When returning internationally:

  • Mark the package as "RETURNED GOODS" on customs forms
  • Include the RMA number on all documentation
  • Declare the value as $0 or the original price minus shipping
  • Keep all shipping documentation and tracking

Additional Costs

International customers are responsible for:

  • Return shipping costs (unless due to our error)
  • Customs duties and taxes on returned items
  • Any brokerage or handling fees

Refunds for International Returns

For international refunds:

  • Refunds are issued in USD
  • Currency conversion rates apply
  • Bank fees for international transfers may apply
  • Original shipping costs are not refundable

9. Store Credit Option

Instead of a refund to your original payment method, you may choose store credit:

Benefits of Store Credit

  • Instant issuance: Available immediately after return approval
  • No expiration: Use anytime in the future
  • Free shipping: Often included with store credit promotions
  • Bonus credit: Sometimes we offer extra credit as a thank you

How Store Credit Works

  1. Request store credit during the return process
  2. Receive a unique store credit code via email
  3. Apply the code at checkout on your next purchase
  4. Use partial amounts - remaining credit stays available

10. Non-Returnable Situations

Some situations may affect your return eligibility:

Common Issues

  • Worn or washed items: Any signs of wear make items non-returnable
  • Missing tags: Items without original tags cannot be returned
  • Altered items: Hemmed, tailored, or modified apparel
  • Pet hair or odors: Items with pet hair or strong odors
  • Late returns: Returns initiated after 30 days

If Your Return is Denied

If we cannot accept your return, we will:

  • Explain the reason for denial
  • Offer to ship the item back to you (at your cost)
  • Provide alternatives if available
  • Keep the item for 30 days before disposal

Need Help With a Return?

Our returns team is here to assist you with any return questions or issues.
