Product Description

The SilkSpeed Summer Performance Riding Pants are crafted using breathable, quick-dry microfibers that provide unmatched comfort during warm-weather training. Designed with 4-way stretch and ergonomic panel construction, these pants adapt to your movement, enhancing posture and stability in every ride.

Their lightweight feel, reinforced seams, and abrasion-resistant zones make them ideal for both casual riders and professionals seeking consistency, durability, and premium performance.
