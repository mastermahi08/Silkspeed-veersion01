Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 7-15 business days, express shipping 3-7 days. International delivery times vary by destination.

What is your return policy?

We offer 30-day returns on unworn items with original tags. Return shipping is the customer's responsibility.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide with reliable logistics partners.

How do I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships, which works on 17track.com.
