Cookie Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to transparency about how cookies are used on the SilkSpeed website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide insights into how the website is used.

SilkSpeed Race Wear uses cookies to ensure our website operates efficiently, securely, and consistently for users worldwide.

Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be disabled.

  • Website security
  • Page navigation
  • Shopping cart and checkout
  • Order tracking access

Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the website so we can improve performance and usability.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies allow the website to remember your preferences, such as language or region, to enhance your browsing experience.

Marketing & Advertising Cookies

These cookies may be used to deliver relevant advertisements or measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns. They are used only with your consent, where required by law.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by trusted third-party services that help us operate our website, including:

  • Payment gateways
  • Analytics providers
  • Shipping and order tracking partners

These cookies are governed by the privacy policies of their respective providers.

Your Cookie Choices

When you first visit our website, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner allowing you to accept, reject, or customize your cookie preferences.

You may change or withdraw your consent at any time using your browser settings or by reopening the cookie preferences tool.

Data Privacy & Security

Cookies used on this website do not store sensitive personal data such as passwords or payment details. All cookie-related data is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect legal or operational changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
